KIHON VR Karate Sensei

Reimagining online karate training

KIHON represents DruvaTalks' venture into immersive educational technology. By combining virtual reality with martial arts instruction, we're exploring how technology can enhance traditional learning methods and make expert instruction accessible beyond physical boundaries.

Key Innovation Areas:

  • Online karate training focus: Designed specifically to address the challenges of remote martial arts instruction
  • VR-enabled instruction: Immersive visual demonstrations that help learners understand complex movements
  • Instructor-guided learning logic: Adaptive guidance systems that support practice beyond the dojo

Developed as part of DruvaTalks innovation initiatives to push the boundaries of accessible education.
